Well, what is Helping Hands?

Modern technology can be confusing to use and can bring up many questions. Helping Hands combines human interaction and technological innovation, including screen-share technology, to bring you solutions to your technological problems. With only a click of a button, we connect you with one of our many technology specialists, who will guide you through any technological problem. Just open up our app, click a button, and ask away!

Hmm, this is different. How do I even use it?

Download the app for free on the Android/ios market.
Open the app and login (or sign up).
Select a catagory (button) and be immmediately connected to a live specialist
It’s as simple as that.

Financing our Business

The Helping Hands service is based on a 30 day free trial, then we will require the purchase of a very affordable subscription of $8 per month. We are able to keep costs this low by initially limiting the scope of out expertise, only offering technical support for phones and tablets.
